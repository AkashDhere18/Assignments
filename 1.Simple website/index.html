Republic of India

Though India became a free nation on August 15, 1947, it declared itself a Sovereign, Democratic and Republic state with the adoption of the Constitution on January 26, 1950.

A salute of 21 guns and the unfurling of the Indian National Flag by Dr. Rajendra Prasad heralded the historic birth of the Indian Republic on that day. Thereafter 26th of January was decreed a national holiday and was recognised as the Republic Day of India.

The Constitution gave the citizens of India the power to choose their own government and paved the way for democracy. Dr. Rajendra Prasad took oath as the first President of India.


Facts:-

  • Republic Day is celebrated every year on 26 January.
  • The Constitution of India came into force in 1950.
  • Dr. B. R. Ambedkar was the chief architect of the Indian Constitution.
  • A grand parade is held at Rajpath (Kartavya Path), New Delhi.
  • Republic Day represents unity, democracy, and freedom.
